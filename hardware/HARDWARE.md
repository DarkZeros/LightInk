# About hardware

The hardware is the mixture of [Case](hardware/case/CASE.md), [PCB](hardware/pcb/PCB.md) and external components.

For the first 2 please visit those pages.

# External Hardware

Mainly it is the Display (0154BN-D67 or 1.54'' B/W 200x200), based on SSD1681
https://www.good-display.com/product/556.html
Any similar one will work, from Waveshare or other manufacturer.

You will also need the solar panel:
Panasonic Amorton 5815 (31.2mm x 10.8mm x 1.1mm cell), 4.5V-5.2V 1.1mA-2.5mA
I bought a pack of 20 from ChipOneStop a while ago.

The vibration motors, any will do. Use the same as watchy uses.
"Seeded studio Mini vibration motor 2.0mm"

Also for piezo speaker, any will do but I used the 25mm disk piezo speaker.

Regarding the battery, use a 50mAh battery LiPo, that is as flat as possible,
and fits in the 30x30mm space. I used a 20x10mm one.

Then, the LED light you can use any LED that runs at 3V.
